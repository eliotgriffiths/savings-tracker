🖥 Browser Storage

Store data only in this browser's local storage. No account or internet connection needed.

⚠️ Risks
Your data will be permanently lost if you clear your browser data, uninstall the browser, or switch devices. There is no backup file you can copy or recover from.

Storage Modes

🔗 Google Drive Sync Recommended

Your data is saved as a private JSON file in your own Google Drive. It syncs automatically across all your devices — phone, tablet, and desktop. If you clear your browser data or switch devices, nothing is lost.

💾 Local Only This device

Your data is stored only in your browser's local storage on this device. No account or internet connection is needed, but your data won't be accessible on other devices and will be lost if you clear your browser data.

🔐 Encryption

Password-encrypts your backup files using AES-256. A recovery key file is downloaded when you set a password — keep it safe. Without it, a forgotten password means your backup cannot be recovered.

🔐 Set backup password

Your backup files will be encrypted with this password.

⚠️ Important: If you forget this password and lose your recovery key, your backups cannot be recovered. There is no reset option.
A vault-recovery.json file will be downloaded automatically. Store it somewhere safe — it can unlock your backup if you forget your password.

🔐 Encrypted backup

Your backup file is password-protected. Enter your password to load your data.

Forgot your password?

Use your vault-recovery.json file to unlock. Encryption will be disabled so you can set a new password.
